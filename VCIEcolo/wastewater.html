Waste Water Odor Control

Municipal wastewater treatment plants (MWTP) provide an essential community service that is vital for the protection of public health and the environment in which we live. Unfortunately, these services and facilities bring with them numerous disruptive odorous compounds. Through the careful application, VCIEcolo products can eliminate the creation and spread of odors in several areas such as:

  • Screen/Press room odors can be treated with waterless XStreme Vapor Technology.
  • Perimeter spraying to reduce off-site complaints
  • Tank/Lagoon foaming to reduce odor particle creation
  • Lift Stations can be treated with neutralizers, reactants and biological odor suppressants to meet the systems needs.
  • Sludge handling can be improved through BioStreme applications as well as AirSolution misting and fogging to neutralize od

With the world’s demand for clean water increasing at an exponential rate, the need for adequate odor control from wastewater will rise with it. VCIEcolo is here to meet your wastewater odor control needs.
